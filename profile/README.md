# GSAS Micro Systems

**Catalyzing Embedded Innovation In India**

GSAS Micro Systems is India's embedded technology partner — delivering engineering tools, safety-certified software, and embedded engineering services to 4,200+ engineering teams since 2010. Headquartered in Bengaluru with six regional offices across India, we combine two decades years of embedded domain expertise with the reach and partnerships to serve India's most demanding programmes.

We work at the intersection of embedded software, hardware design, functional safety, and FPGA engineering — from early-stage architecture to production deployment.

---

## Engineering Services

### Embedded Systems
Firmware development, BSP bring-up, RTOS integration (FreeRTOS, embOS, QNX), communication stack implementation (CAN, LIN, CAN-FD, BLE, Ethernet/IP), bootloader design, and driver development for microcontrollers and application processors across automotive, aerospace, industrial, and medical platforms.

### Functional Safety & Compliance
Full safety lifecycle engineering across ISO 26262 (automotive, up to ASIL-D), IEC 61508 (industrial), and DO-178C (avionics). AUTOSAR architecture and SWC development, ASPICE process consulting, HARA, FTA, FMEA, safety case development, and tool qualification. Requirements engineering, traceability management, and safety documentation from concept to production release.

### FPGA Design & Verification
RTL design in VHDL, Verilog, and SystemVerilog. IP core development and integration, timing closure, and synthesis on Xilinx Zynq/UltraScale+ and Intel Quartus platforms. UVM testbench development, formal verification, functional simulation, and coverage-driven verification. SoC integration — Zynq PS-PL architecture, AXI interconnect, embedded Linux on FPGA, and high-speed interface bring-up: PCIe, DDR, JESD204B.

### Hardware Engineering
Schematic capture, high-speed PCB layout, signal integrity analysis, power architecture, DFM/DFT, and full fab output for mission-critical hardware platforms. Reference board design, prototype validation, and hardware design review as a service.

### Security Engineering
Secure boot implementation, TrustZone, embedded cryptography (Mbed TLS, wolfSSL), UNECE R155, EU CRA compliance, and threat modelling for connected automotive and industrial systems.

### Testing & Validation
Unit and integration testing (TESSY), MISRA C/C++ and CERT C static analysis, HIL and SIL simulation environments, requirements-based testing, and system-level validation for safety-critical software. Code coverage analysis and qualification evidence generation.

### T&M Integration & Production
Test bench automation, production flash programming (SEGGER Flasher, gang programmers), power profiling and energy characterisation (Joulescope), protocol analysis (CAN, I2C, SPI, USB), and automated validation infrastructure for manufacturing and R&D environments.

### Training & Enablement
Tool workshops, functional safety certification courses, embedded systems training, AUTOSAR/ASPICE programmes, and FPGA/UVM training for engineering teams. Corporate training programmes tailored to automotive, aerospace, and industrial domains.
