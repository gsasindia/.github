# GSAS Micro Systems

**Precision by Design. Intelligence by Standard.**

Founded in 2010, GSAS Micro Systems is an embedded systems engineering company headquartered in Bangalore, India. We deliver safety-critical engineering services and technology solutions to some of India's most demanding programmes — from automotive domain controllers to aerospace flight systems.

We operate across six locations in India, partnering with 4,000+ engineering teams in automotive, aerospace, defence, healthcare, and industrial sectors.

---

## What We Do

**Embedded Systems Engineering**
Firmware development, BSP bring-up, RTOS integration, communication stacks (CAN, LIN, BLE, Ethernet), and bootloader design for safety-critical applications.

**Functional Safety & Compliance**
Full safety lifecycle engineering across ISO 26262 (automotive), IEC 61508 (industrial), and DO-178C (avionics). AUTOSAR architecture, ASPICE process consulting, HARA, FTA, and safety case development.

**FPGA Design & Verification**
RTL design in VHDL, Verilog, and SystemVerilog. UVM testbench development, formal verification, and SoC integration on Xilinx Zynq and Intel platforms. High-speed interface bring-up — PCIe, DDR, JESD204B.

**Hardware Engineering**
Schematic design, high-speed PCB layout, signal integrity analysis, DFM/DFT, and full fab output for mission-critical hardware platforms.

**Testing & Validation**
Unit and integration testing, MISRA/CERT C static analysis, HIL/SIL simulation, and system-level validation for safety-critical software.

**Security Engineering**
Secure boot, TrustZone, embedded cryptography, and UNECE R155 / EU CRA compliance for connected automotive and industrial systems.
